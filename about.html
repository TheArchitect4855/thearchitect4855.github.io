Who am I?

My name is Kurtis Knodel. I was born in Burnaby, BC, Canada, on May 12th 2002. After some moving around when I was young, myself and my family finally settled in Kelowna, where I've lived and went to school ever since.

Ever since I was young, I was amazed by computers. I thought they were the coolest thing, basically ever. Finally, when I was 11 years old, I decided I wanted to make a video game. After a lot of "arguing" with my parents, I convinced them to let me download Unity (then Unity3D) onto our home PC and give it a go.

I fell in love.

From there, I taught myself how to program, how to bend the computer to my will, and I made small games just for fun. After that, I expanded my horizons into software development. When I was 16, I started freelancing to make some extra cash, and in the years before that I would work on "revshare gamedev teams" just because I enjoyed it. When I was 15, I worked on a "professional" team that almost got a publishing deal, but that fell apart pretty quick. I was skeptical about it then, and to this day I still am.
Since then I've been doing freelancing and making games in my free time, and now I'm just about to start University to study for a Computer Science degree.
